# Channel instrument icons — attribution

The instrument icons in this directory (`drums`, `perc`, `bass`, `elec`, `keys`,
`synth`, `vox`, `strings`, `md`) are derived from the **Behringer X32 scribble-strip
icon set**:

- Source: https://github.com/mamarguerat/behringer-icons
- Original BMP icons & naming: Patrick-Gilles Maillot
- SVG packaging: mamarguerat
- License: **Apache-2.0** (same license as GraceTracks)

These SVGs were produced by vectorising the source 64×64 BMPs with `potrace`
(see `scripts/convert-x32-icons.sh`) and rewriting the fill to `currentColor`
so they inherit the app theme. The source bitmaps live in `/X32-icons/`.

The remaining channel icons (`click`, `ambient`, `master`) and all transport /
UI icons come from [Lucide](https://lucide.dev) (ISC).
